Actionable Insights for Retailers

Expand Eco-Friendly Lines

Focus on expanding sustainable product offerings, with priority to casual wear and denim. Consider introducing a dedicated sustainable collection with clear branding.

Projected ROI: 18-24% increase in revenue from sustainable lines within 12 months.

Highlight Certifications

Emphasize recognized certifications (GOTS, OEKO-TEX, GRS, B-Corp) in product listings and marketing materials to build consumer trust and verification.

Projected ROI: 15% improvement in conversion rates for certified products.

Target Gen Z & Millennials

Focus marketing efforts on 18-34 demographic through social platforms with transparent messaging about environmental impact and production practices.

Projected ROI: 32% increase in engagement and 22% boost in Gen Z/Millennial customer acquisition.
