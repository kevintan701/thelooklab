About Elena

Elena Rodriguez is a New York-based fashion designer known for her minimal, sustainable approach to modern clothing. With a background in architecture, Elena brings structural precision and clean lines to her designs, creating pieces that are both timeless and contemporary.

After graduating from Parsons School of Design in 2015, Elena worked with several established designers before launching her eponymous label in 2018. Her commitment to ethical production and sustainable materials has earned her recognition in the industry, including the Emerging Designer Award in 2020.

Elena's designs have been featured in Vogue, Elle, and Harper's Bazaar, and worn by celebrities including Emma Stone and Zendaya. Her brand continues to grow with a focus on quality craftsmanship and thoughtful, versatile designs for the modern consumer.
